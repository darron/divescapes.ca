The Alberta Underwater Council is proud to present the Divescapes Conference and Exhibition. Divescapes has had several outstanding shows in both Edmonton and Calgary recently. With world class presenters, exhibitors, workshops & prizes for the benefit & enjoyment of Alberta’s scuba divers!

Next Divescapes Scuba Conference is being planned for OCTOBER 23, 24, 25, 2020 in Edmonton at the River Cree Resort & Casino. Join the festivities to help celebrate AUC's 60th Anniversary and Divescapes' 25th in 2020.

Divescapes' volunteer committee members needed. If interested, contact Cathie at the AUC office soon.

Divescapes Conference mandate includes striving to promote safe & environmentally responsible diving and provide all participants with the opportunity to partner with other industry organizations like participating local dive centres, government agencies, tourism, travel, clubs, service groups, etc., to develop a network of resources for underwater sports & scuba diving in Alberta & around the world.
